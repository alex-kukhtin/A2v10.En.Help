SheetTreeSection Element

All elements of the sheets

Represents a section for placing hierarchical data. Binds to a data source recursively.

Designated for binding to hierarchical models or models with grouping. Such models are in fact recursive.

Inherits: SheetSection : UIElement : UIElementBase
